Hi, I'm Tahifz Makhdum Samit, and I'm excited to be a part of the Adarsh Samad Math Club for the 2023-2024 school year. I've always been passionate about math, and I'm looking forward to learning more about it with my fellow students.

One of my proudest achievements as a member of the club was when we renovated the abandoned auditorium in just a few hours. It was a lot of hard work, but it was also a lot of fun working together with everyone. We all learned a lot about teamwork and cooperation, and we also got to know each other better.

I'm also proud of the fact that we organized this event without any support from the teachers. It showed that we are a self-motivated and independent group of students who are capable of achieving great things.

I'm looking forward to participating in all of the club's activities this year, including the math competitions, the tutoring sessions, and the field trips. I'm also excited to meet new people and make new friends.

I would like to welcome all the new members to the Adarsh Samad Math Club. I hope you have a great time learning and growing with us. I know that I will!
