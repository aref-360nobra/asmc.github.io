Sidratul Suhan

Coordinator(2023-2024)

Welcome, fellow math enthusiasts and curious minds! I'm Sidratul Suhan, and I'm absolutely thrilled to be your coordinator for the Adarsha Samad Math Club for the academic year 2023-2024. Let me take you on a brief journey through my experiences and the remarkable achievements we've accomplished together.

My journey with the math club started in eighth grade, where I stumbled upon the world of 'clubbing' and found my true passion for mathematics. Learning isn't just about textbooks; it's about connecting with students, engaging their attention, and making learning enjoyable. This invaluable experience has helped me foster an environment where knowledge and fun come together seamlessly.

One of our proudest achievements was the Math Olympiad we organized, in collaboration with both our senior and junior members. The second Adarsha Samad Math Olympiad in 2022 was a turning point, providing us with an incredible learning experience. Inspired by its success, we decided to spread our wings even further. The third Math Olympiad expanded beyond our school's walls, inviting students from other schools. The excitement of visiting different schools, explaining the Olympiad, and forming unforgettable bonds has left an indelible mark on all of us.

Preparing for the event was no small feat. We transformed our school auditorium into a vibrant hub of mathematical exploration, a testament to our teamwork and determination. The third Adarsha Samad Math Olympiad, graced by around 300 students, was a resounding success, etching memories that will last a lifetime.

As you embark on your journey with the Adarsha Samad Math Club, I invite you to embrace the spirit of collaboration, curiosity, and creativity. Together, we'll explore the captivating world of mathematics, forge lasting friendships, and make this academic year truly exceptional. Here's to new beginnings and countless math-filled adventures ahead!
